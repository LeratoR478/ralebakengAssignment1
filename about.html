Story of the Roof

The legend, the people, and the spirit behind this unforgettable mountain adventure

History of Sani Pass

Sani Pass is one of the most famous mountain routes linking Lesotho and South Africa. Known for its dramatic curves, steep climbs, and unforgettable scenery, it has become a top destination for travellers who love nature, adventure, and highland exploration.

Over the years, Sani Pass has attracted off-road drivers, photographers, hikers, and cultural tourists from around the world. The route is more than just a mountain pass; it is a symbol of Lesotho’s natural beauty and adventurous tourism identity.

Why This Event Exists

The Sani Pass 4x4 Experience was created to promote tourism in Lesotho and give visitors a chance to enjoy the highlands through adventure, culture, and scenic discovery. The event celebrates the excitement of 4x4 travel while also introducing guests to Basotho hospitality, local traditions, music, and storytelling.

It is designed to attract both local and international tourists while highlighting Lesotho as a powerful tourism destination with unique landscapes and memorable experiences.
